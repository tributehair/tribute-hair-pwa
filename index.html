Our Story

Made with Purpose

Tribute Hair & Body Care is a Black-owned beauty brand rooted in Mansfield, Texas. We formulate premium natural products for real hair textures — from 3C to 4C and everything in between.

Every product is cruelty-free and made with carefully chosen natural and naturally-derived ingredients. We also supply salons, boutiques, braiders, and locticians wholesale through our Texas Wholesale portal.
