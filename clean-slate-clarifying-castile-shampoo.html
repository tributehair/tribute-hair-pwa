How to Use

  1. 01 Wet hair thoroughly. For maximum buildup removal, use warm water to open the cuticle before applying.
  2. 02 Apply to scalp and hair and massage gently for 1–2 minutes. The castile base lathers differently than sulfate shampoos — work it in well.
  3. 03 Rinse thoroughly. For heavy buildup, double cleanse — the second pass will lather more fully once initial residue is cleared.
  4. 04 Optional ACV rinse: Mix 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar with 1 cup water and pour through hair. This restores scalp pH after castile cleansing.
  5. 05 Follow with Velvet Dew Conditioner to replenish moisture and restore softness after the deep cleanse.

Usage frequency: Once monthly as a reset, or every 2–4 weeks for scalp-prone and heavy product users. Not recommended for daily use.
