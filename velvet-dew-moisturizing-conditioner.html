How to Use

  1. 01 Apply after shampooing — squeeze excess water from hair, then apply Velvet Dew from mid-lengths to ends.
  2. 02 Detangle gently with fingers or a wide-tooth comb while conditioner is in hair for maximum slip and minimal breakage.
  3. 03 Leave on 2–5 minutes (or up to 15 minutes under heat for deep conditioning effect).
  4. 04 Rinse thoroughly with cool water to seal the cuticle and lock in the 72-hour moisture complex.
  5. 05 Style as desired. For extra moisture, apply a leave-in conditioner or oil to damp hair before styling.

Pro tip: For a deep conditioning treatment, apply to dry hair, cover with a plastic cap, and leave on for 20–30 minutes before rinsing.
