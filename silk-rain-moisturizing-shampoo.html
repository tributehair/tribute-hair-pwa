How to Use

  1. 01 Wet hair thoroughly with warm water — this opens the cuticle and maximizes ingredient penetration.
  2. 02 Apply a generous pump to the scalp and work through the length of your hair using fingertips (not nails).
  3. 03 Lather and massage for 1–2 minutes, focusing on the scalp. Allow the foam to travel through the ends.
  4. 04 Rinse thoroughly with cool or warm water until water runs completely clear.
  5. 05 Follow with Velvet Dew Moisturizing Conditioner for maximum hydration and manageability.

Pro tip: For heavily product-laden hair, double cleanse — first pass removes buildup, second pass delivers full ingredient benefits.
